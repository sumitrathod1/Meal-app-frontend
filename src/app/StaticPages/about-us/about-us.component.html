About Us

Rishabh Software was founded in the year 2000 by Mr. & Mrs. Raju Shah with a simple thought of delivering exceptional value to global customers. From the start, the vision was clear – to empower businesses with world-class software solutions. They did this by tapping into local talent in their home state of Gujarat by building a platform for budding software developers to grow and nurture their careers. At Rishabh, we not only provide your business with exceptional enterprise-grade applications but go above and beyond. With 1200+ successful projects and a process-driven mindset, we’ve got your back. Our repeat clientele speaks volumes for our values and ethics in all our dealings – be it software development across web, mobile, analytics, cloud, or through our engineering and BPO services. We believe in doing the best for our clients because WE CARE.
